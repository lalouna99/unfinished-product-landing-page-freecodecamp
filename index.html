Audience Reviews

10/10 a good book to snuggle up with. Love the slice of life fantasy style and not having romance being the fore front. - Elijah Zamora

This is a fantastic book. Ever wonder what adventures do when they are unable to adventure anymore? Baldree answers this question with a well-developed storyline, characters, and atmosphere. I listened to the audiobook, read by him, and I finished it quickly, staying up a few hours extra each night to get through it. I highly recommend this book to anyone who loves fantasy and wants a new style of adventure. - Joshua Furtado

I've been an avid reader since I was a child in the 80's, and fantasy has always been my favored genre. I've read several hundred fantasy books over the decades, and Legends & Lattes is my new favorite fantasy book. Despite the relatively low stakes, the story is compelling, and the main character is likeable and relatable. I enjoy adventure and high stakes as much as the next reader, but this was a breath of fresh air, and I highly recommend it. - Mori Convallaria
